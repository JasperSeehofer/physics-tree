    **Phase files** — each phase-{N}.md must have H2 headings matching the `requires` list (Title Case form of the snake_case keys). Content can be concise but must be substantive enough to be non-trivial (not just placeholder "TODO" text). Use real kinematics physics content drawn from the existing kinematics.md flat file where applicable.

    **phase-0.md** (Schema Activation): H2 headings `## Recall Prompt`, `## Linkage Map`, `## Wonder Hook`. Content about connecting prior knowledge of position, velocity, and motion.

    **phase-1.md** (Productive Struggle): H2 headings `## Struggle Problem`, `## Solution Capture`, `## Gap Reveal`. A problem the learner cannot fully solve with prerequisite knowledge alone (e.g., predicting position from non-constant velocity without integration).

    **phase-2.md** (Concreteness Fading): H2 headings `## Concrete Stage`, `## Bridging Stage`, `## Abstract Stage`, `## Derivation`. From physical motion examples to formal kinematic equations, including derivation of v = v0 + at and x = x0 + v0*t + (1/2)at^2.

    **phase-3.md** (Worked Examples): H2 headings `## Full Example`, `## Partially Faded Example`, `## Mostly Faded Example`. Progressively remove scaffolding from a kinematics problem.

    **phase-4.md** (Self Explanation): H2 headings `## Self Explanation Prompt`, `## Reflection Questions`. Prompts asking learner to explain why certain kinematic relationships hold.

    **phase-5.md** (Retrieval Check): H2 heading `## Quiz`. At least 2 quiz blocks in the format from docs/content-spec.md.

    **phase-6.md** (Spaced Return): H2 headings `## Spaced Prompt`, `## Interleaving Problem`. A recall prompt and a problem mixing kinematics with prerequisite concepts.

    IMPORTANT: Each phase file should have real physics content (formulas in LaTeX, actual example problems, genuine quiz questions). This will be used as the gold-standard fixture for the ingest pipeline.
